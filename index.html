About GM

Grand Master Thomas Caulfield

Train with Grand Master Thomas Caulfield III and his ELITE team to learn the true meaning of Hanmi Kwan Tang Soo Do Martial Arts. GM Caulfield was born into the Martial Arts family through his father; Former creator and Grand Master Thomas Caulfield Jr. Training in all over Asia GM Caulfield returned to the United States to teach at Luke Air force base. GM Caulfield has been teaching since the 1980s.

Throughout all these years Grand Master Caulfield has mastered these arts from the following: Grand Master Thomas Caulfield Jr, Grand Master Darryl D Khalid, Grand Master Jae Chul Shin, Grand Master Wilson E. "Nonong" Vinas, Sensei Dyrchz, Sensei Lehninger, Sifu Yi & Many more.

FUN FACT: Supreme Grand Master Darryl Khalid was the body guard of famous boxer Muhammad Ali
